About the Team

The Kazakhstan national football team represents Kazakhstan in men's international football and is governed by the Kazakhstan Football Federation (KFF). After the dissolution of the Soviet Union, Kazakhstan initially joined the Asian Football Confederation (AFC) before moving to the Union of European Football Associations (UEFA) in 2002.

FIFA World Cup Qualifiers

The team actively participates in the European qualification campaigns for the FIFA World Cup. They compete against other UEFA member nations for a spot in the prestigious tournament.

  • Competed in qualifiers for 2006, 2010, 2014, 2018, and 2022 World Cups as a UEFA member.
  • Previously competed in AFC qualifiers for the 1998 and 2002 World Cups.
  • Matches provide valuable international experience against strong European opposition.

UEFA Euro Qualifiers

Kazakhstan is a regular participant in the qualification process for the UEFA European Championship. They compete in qualifying groups aiming to secure a place among Europe's elite national teams.

  • Competed in qualifiers for Euro 2008, 2012, 2016, 2020, and 2024.
  • Showed significant improvement in the Euro 2024 qualifiers, finishing close to a playoff spot.
  • These campaigns are crucial for the development of football in Kazakhstan.
