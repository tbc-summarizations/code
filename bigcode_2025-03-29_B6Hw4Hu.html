GEORGE FLOYD: The Game

TIMELINE

The game starts with 🚶🏾 George Floyd waking up in an unknown city, realizing he must fight through waves of enemies 🔥 to reclaim justice and peace.

BOSSES

🎵Tutorial Boss: Drake (The Rap Titan)

Drake tries to convince George to abandon his mission, using charisma and distractions (🎶🕺), but ultimately fails.

After defeating Drake, his ghost (👻🎵) will occasionally spawn as an enemy, trying to distract you with dance moves and music.

🎤First Boss: 2Pac (The Rap Legend)

Tupac returns from the afterlife, testing George's resolve. The battle is intense, filled with lyrical attacks (📜🗣️).

🤖Second Boss: Siri (The AI Overlord)

Siri manipulates the battlefield, altering terrain () and summoning tech-based minions (⚙️) to confuse and slow George.

🎧Third Boss: JPEGMAFIA (The Chaotic Producer)

A fight filled with unpredictable attacks and sound-based weaponry, with his beats turning into projectiles (💥🎶).

🏛️Fourth Boss: Ronald Reagan (The Ghost of the Past)

Reagan’s spirit attempts to use historical policies as attacks, summoning trickle-down economic waves (📉💸) to push George back.

After this fight, ghostly figures (👻) will occasionally appear on the map to slow progress.

🚨Fifth Boss: Smoke Detector (The Alarm of Oppression)

An anthropomorphic smoke detector that constantly rings (BEEP! BEEP!), stunning George and limiting visibility (🌫️).

🚀Sixth Boss: Elon Musk (The Tech Mogul)

Musk arrives in a cybernetic suit (🤖), deploying drones (🛸) and space-tech weapons (⚡🛰️) against George.

💰Seventh Boss: Donald Trump (The Former President)

Trump rides in on a golden escalator (▼), throwing executive orders (📄) and tweets (🐦) as attacks.

📜Eighth Boss: Jefferson Davis (The Confederate Leader)

Summoning ghosts of the Confederacy (👻🎖️), Davis attempts to drag George into the past.

👮Ninth Boss: The Police (The Enforcers)

A swarm battle where George faces waves of police officers (👮‍♂️👮‍♀️) with riot shields (🛡️) and tasers (⚡).

🔥Tenth Boss: The Klan (The Hate Group)

The KKK leaders emerge, using fire (🔥) and fear (😨) as their primary weapons.

🛡️Final Boss: Derek Chauvin (The Ultimate Oppressor)

The most difficult battle, where Chauvin attempts to physically and symbolically suppress George (✋膝). Only through ultimate resistance (💪) and countering his moves can George defeat him.

Gameplay Features

  • 📱Mobile Support: Designed with touchscreen controls and fluid mechanics.
  • ⚔️Combat System: A mix of melee (👊) and ranged () attacks with power-ups (⭐).
  • ⬆️Upgrade System: Unlock new abilities after each boss fight.
  • 🗺️Map Progression: Changes after key battles, introducing new hazards (⚠️).
  • ✨Special Abilities:
    • 🌊 "Justice Wave"
    • 💣 "Truth Bomb"
    • 🛡️ "Resilience Shield"
  • 🔄Mini-bosses: Random enemy spawns from previous bosses (👻🎵, 👻).

This game is a journey through struggle, resistance, and ultimate victory.
