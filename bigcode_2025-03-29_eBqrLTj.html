It's-a me, Mario!

Mario is the iconic Italian plumber protagonist of the long-running and highly successful Nintendo video game franchise of the same name. Created by Japanese video game designer Shigeru Miyamoto, Mario is one of the most famous characters in video game history.

About Mario

First appearing in the 1981 arcade game Donkey Kong as "Jumpman", Mario later became the star of his own series, starting with Super Mario Bros. in 1985. He is known for his signature red hat and shirt, blue overalls, white gloves, brown shoes, and prominent mustache. Mario resides in the Mushroom Kingdom and is often tasked with rescuing Princess Peach from the villainous Bowser.

Throughout his adventures, Mario utilizes various power-ups, such as the Super Mushroom, Fire Flower, and Super Star, granting him special abilities like size alteration, projectile attacks, and temporary invincibility. He is often accompanied by his fraternal twin brother, Luigi.
